Wireframe Notes

Header: Standard header with logo and primary navigation (e.g., Course Details, About).

Hero: Above the fold. Must grab attention immediately with a strong headline and a clear CTA.

Features: A quick, scannable section highlighting the 3 main benefits of the course.

Modules: The core of the page. Should use an accordion to avoid a long wall of text. Each item expands to show details.

Testimonials: Social proof section to build trust and credibility.

Final CTA: A last chance to convert the user after they've seen all the information.

Footer: Standard footer with legal links, social media, etc.
